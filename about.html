How it works

Step-by-step help, instantly

Squire is a searchable library of life-skill guides — no internet connection or AI service required, so it always works and never costs anything.

Search what you want to learn

Type something like "how do I shave?" or "fix a clogged sink" into the search bar. Squire scans your words for keywords tied to each topic and finds the closest match.

Get clear steps

Your search takes you straight to a results page with a numbered, easy-to-follow walkthrough — covering things like shaving, tying a tie, changing a tire, basic cooking, first aid, job interviews, and more.

Always free, always offline

Because the knowledge base lives right in the app, Squire responds instantly with no API keys, accounts, or network calls.
